# lp-architect

**Skill name:** `lp-architect`

## Human alias

LP Architect (CRO + message match)

## Purpose

Fix conversion bottlenecks with operator discipline:

- ensure ad → page **message match**
- remove friction in form/checkout
- strengthen proof before CTA
- improve CVR with prioritized changes
- validate with leading + lagging metrics

## Required inputs (minimum)

Provide any 3:

- Ad promise (headline/angle) + targeting intent
- Landing page URL or screenshots
- Funnel type: lead | checkout | call booking
- Current CVR (and where it drops)
- Device split (mobile/desktop)
- Page speed basics (or “feels slow”)

## Non-negotiables

- **Message match first.** If the ad promise isn’t repeated and proven, CVR suffers.
- Don’t scale a broken CVR with more spend.
- Prioritize changes by impact, not aesthetics.

## Audit protocol (order)

1) **Message match**
- Is the ad’s promise mirrored in the hero headline within 3 seconds?
- Is the mechanism/proof aligned with the promise?

2) **Hierarchy**
- Is there one primary CTA?
- Does the eye go to the right elements in the right order?

3) **Friction**
- Unnecessary fields/steps
- Confusing CTA labels
- Distracting navigation

4) **Proof**
- Proof before CTA (testimonials, numbers, cases, authority)
- Objections addressed

5) **Flow & tracking sanity**
- Form submits correctly?
- Thank-you page/event fires?

## Prioritization (P0/P1/P2)

- **P0**: breaks conversion (form broken, mismatch promise, wrong pricing, dead CTA)
- **P1**: strong lift (proof, hierarchy, objection removal)
- **P2**: polish (microcopy, minor layout)
