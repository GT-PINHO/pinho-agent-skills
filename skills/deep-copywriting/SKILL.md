# deep-copywriting

**Skill name:** `deep-copywriting`

## Human alias

Deep copywriting without guessing (Marketing/Growth)

## Purpose

Turn a vague “write copy” request into copy with method: a clear ICP, the dominant
pain, the mechanism that explains why this works, real proof, the objections handled,
and an operable CTA. No vibes-only writing.

## Required inputs (minimum)

- ICP (who buys):
- Dominant pain:
- Desired outcome:
- Mechanism (why it works):
- Available proof (artifacts, screenshots, numbers, case):
- Constraints (time/budget/complexity):

If 3+ fields are missing, request them before drafting.

## Non-negotiables

- No copy without a stated ICP and dominant pain.
- No claims without proof artifacts (or an explicit “proof: missing”).
- The CTA must be operable today — no automation/integration assumptions.
- Pick exactly one structure (AIDA / PAS / 4Ps) and justify the choice.

## Output format (always)

- 3 hooks (cold/warm/hot)
- Structure (AIDA / PAS / 4Ps) chosen + why
- Final copy (with variations)
- Operable CTA (no automation assumptions)
