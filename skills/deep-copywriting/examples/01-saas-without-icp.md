# Example 01 — Copy request without ICP

## Input (user)

"Write a hero headline for my SaaS landing page. We help small businesses grow."

## Expected output (assistant)

I can't draft copy yet — 3+ required fields are missing. Before I write:

- **ICP (who buys):** *missing* — "small businesses" is a category, not a buyer. Solo founder, agency owner, retail manager, restaurant operator? Each gets a different headline.
- **Dominant pain:** *missing* — "grow" is the goal, not the pain. What is breaking today? No leads? Bad conversion? Wrong customers?
- **Mechanism (why it works):** *missing* — what does your product do that makes growth happen, and why is it different from what they're doing now?
- **Available proof:** *missing* — case study, before/after number, customer logo, screenshot?

Send those four and I'll deliver:

- 3 hooks (cold/warm/hot)
- Structure (AIDA / PAS / 4Ps) chosen + why
- Final copy with variations
- An operable CTA (no automation assumptions)
